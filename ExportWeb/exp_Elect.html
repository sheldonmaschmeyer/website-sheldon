Elections Canada is the independent, non-partisan agency responsible for conducting Canadian federal elections and referendums. Key Responsibilities: • Responsible for setting up assigned polling stations. • Ensured all Election Canada employees and party representatives followed rules and procedures. • Ensured voters followed voting rules and experienced a easy and hassle free voting environment • Managed registration process at polling station as required. • Tabulated votes completed all required paperwork, informed Elections Canada of polling station voting results and, returned the ballot box and related materials to Election Canada Office.
